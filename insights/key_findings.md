# Key Business Insights

This document summarizes the major insights derived from the Sales and Customer dashboards built using Tableau for the year 2022.

---

# 1. Overall Sales Performance

The company experienced strong business growth in 2022 across key performance metrics.

Key KPIs:
- Total Sales: $609K
- Total Profit: $82K
- Total Quantity Sold: 9,837 units

Year-over-Year Growth:
- Sales increased by 29.5% compared to the previous year.
- Profit increased by 23.3%.
- Total quantity sold increased by 23.3%.

Insight:
The business experienced significant expansion in 2022, with sales growing faster than profit. This suggests that while revenue increased substantially, profit margins may have slightly tightened due to costs or product mix.

---

# 2. Weekly Sales and Profit Trends

Average weekly performance:
- Average weekly sales: approximately $12K
- Average weekly profit: approximately $2K

Observations:
- Several weeks exceeded $25K in sales.
- The highest spike occurred near week 45, where sales approached $30K.
- Profit also showed a major spike during this period.

Insight:
Sales performance accelerated in the later part of the year, indicating strong end-of-year demand or seasonal purchasing behavior.

Business implication:
Companies may benefit from increasing marketing campaigns and inventory during high-demand periods near the end of the year.

---

# 3. Product Subcategory Performance

Sales and profit distribution vary significantly across product categories.

High-performing categories:
- Phones generated strong profits and high sales volume.
- Copiers produced the highest profit among subcategories.
- Accessories also contributed strong profit margins.

Underperforming categories:
- Tables generated a loss despite generating sales.
- Supplies and some smaller categories contributed minimal profit.

Insight:
Certain product categories drive revenue but negatively impact profitability.

Business implication:
- The company should review cost structures or pricing strategies for loss-making categories like Tables.
- High-margin categories such as Copiers and Accessories should be prioritized in marketing and promotions.

---

# 4. Customer Growth and Behavior

Customer KPIs for 2022:

- Total Customers: 638
- Total Orders: 1,315
- Average Sales per Customer: $955

Year-over-Year Growth:
- Customer count increased by 11.3%.
- Sales per customer increased by 16.3%.
- Total orders increased by 26.7%.

Insight:
The business is not only acquiring more customers but also increasing the purchasing value per customer.

Business implication:
Customer engagement and retention strategies appear to be working effectively.

---

# 5. Customer Purchase Distribution

Customer purchase behavior shows a clear pattern.

Distribution of orders per customer:
- 261 customers placed only 1 order
- 191 customers placed 2 orders
- 117 customers placed 3 orders
- Only a small number of customers placed 5+ orders

Insight:
A large portion of customers are one-time buyers.

Business implication:
The company has a strong opportunity to increase revenue by converting one-time buyers into repeat customers through loyalty programs or targeted marketing campaigns.

# 7. Overall Business Value

The dashboards provide stakeholders with clear visibility into:

- Sales performance trends
- Product profitability
- Customer purchasing behavior
- High-value customer segments

By transforming raw transactional data into interactive dashboards, decision-makers can quickly identify growth opportunities and operational inefficiencies.

This enables more effective data-driven decision making across sales, marketing, and customer management.
